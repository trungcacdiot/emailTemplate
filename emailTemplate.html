About Event
The pace at which leaders and policy makers respond to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic will make profound differences to livelihoods, businesses, international trade and travel.

How can we ensure that effective and collective decision-making are based on changing trends and sound scientific understanding in the face of uncertainty?

 Jointly organised by the Singapore Aviation Academy and the National University of Singapore School of Continuing and Lifelong Education,
this webinar will discuss key principles for leadership agility and how we can employ digital assets and scientific research to develop effective plans and anticipate changes by learning from data.
